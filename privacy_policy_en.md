# Privacy Policy for Railtrack

Last updated: May 8, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Railtrack is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your data when you use the Railtrack app.

## 2. Data Collection
The Railtrack app collects the following types of data:

### a. Device Location
The app uses location services (GPS) to identify the nearest train station to your current location. This data is processed exclusively in real-time and locally on the device to provide relevant suggestions; it is NEVER sent to or stored on our servers.

### b. Device and Usage Information
When you voluntarily submit a bug report or feedback through the app, we collect the following information to help us diagnose and resolve issues:
- Android version
- Device brand and model
- App version
- Report timestamp
- Title and description provided by the user

Additionally, third-party advertising services may collect device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) for analysis and ad personalization purposes.

### c. Locally Stored Data
The app stores the following data exclusively on your device to enable its functionality and improve your user experience:
- Stations and trains marked as favorites
- Recent search history
- Theme settings (Light, Dark, or System)
- Onboarding completion status
- Last viewed changelog version
- Notification and auto-refresh preferences

This data resides solely in your smartphone's local memory and is never synchronized with external cloud services managed by us.

## 3. Data Processing and Purpose
Data is processed exclusively to:
- Provide core app functionality (e.g., searching for nearby stations).
- Improve app stability and fix bugs through submitted reports.
- Display relevant advertisements through third-party services.

We use modern encryption methods (HTTPS) for the transmission of any data to our cloud services.

## 4. Third-Party Services
Railtrack uses the following third-party services for its operation:

- **Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore & Remote Config):** Used for receiving bug reports and for dynamic app configuration.
- **Google AdMob:** Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect usage data and device identifiers for ad personalization. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for more details.
- **ViaggiaTreno/Trenitalia APIs:** The app queries public APIs to provide real-time train information. No personal data is sent to these services during queries.

## 5. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Data is shared with the service providers mentioned above only to the extent necessary for app functionality and advertising services. Data may only be shared if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion
- **Local Data:** Preferences and data stored locally can be deleted at any time by clearing the app data from Android settings or by uninstalling the app.
- **Reports:** Reports sent via Firestore are kept for the time necessary to resolve the reported issue. You can request the deletion of any submitted data by contacting us at the address provided below.

## 7. User Rights
In accordance with GDPR and other applicable data protection laws, you have the right to access, rectify, or request the deletion of your data. Since the app does not require account creation, most data is manageable directly through your device.

## 8. Contact Us
For questions or requests regarding your privacy, you can contact us at:
railtrack.dev@gmail.com
